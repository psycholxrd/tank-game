How to Play: 1/8
There are 9 levels in this game.
In order to beat a level, you need
to kill all enemies.
Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move
and Mouse to aim and shoot.
Different enemy projectiles
have different behaviors.
When the enemy projectile hits you,
You get damaged. If you die,
The level resets.
Press Shift to switch
between weapons. The Laser
weapon deals tiny damage
when you're far away
but deals massive damage
when you get close to
the enemy.
The Sniper's weapon damage
doesn't depend on distance.
It takes longer to use, but
it deals good damage. It's
shooting bullets at your mouse
Whenever you eat apples,
you get health, damage,
speed buffs.

Bigger Apples = more buff.
when your health is full,
eating an apple will give
overheal up to 300% of
your initial health.
